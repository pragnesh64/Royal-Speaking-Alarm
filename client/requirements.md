## Notes
- Theme: Paytm-inspired Royal Blue (#002E6E) to Light Blue (#00BAF2) gradients
- Typography: Cambria (Serif) is primary. Text is italicized by default, Numbers must be straight (non-italic).
- Backend: Expects /api/upload for multipart/form-data (voice/images).
- Localization: MVP implements simple state for language selection (Hindi/English/Marathi).
